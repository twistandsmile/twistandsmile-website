üîß Reliability and fixability

This is also an important aspect. When your motorcycle works great, you may not be aware of this, but in case of a problem, having a simple machine is a big plus.

Here the simple motorbikes have the edge, they usually don't need special tools to be worked on and can be easily fixed in case of a problem, because the possible causes are not so many, which greatly facilitate the diagnosis part.

Photo by Tekton

Most of the time, problems don't come from the engine itself. Nowadays, those are strong and reliable. When a problem occurs, the cause is usually from the peripherals, electronics, added electrical accessories and so on. In the best case, only the faulty component stops working, not affecting the rest of the motorbike, and this is alright. So my advice would be, especially if you are traveling : choose a simple, reliable model, from a known brand that have a good network around where you plan to go.
