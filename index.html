Welcome to your new source of motorcycle focused content !

Hi everyone, my name is Clément, I am a 36 years old dad living in Europe, that happen to also be a passionate motorcyclist !

This will be my safe space to share my thoughts and experiences about my motorcycle passion. I always was an introvert, so this will allow me to have a controlled environnement to easily share things with complete strangers !

As I grow older and maybe more mature, I tend to value human communication and interaction more and more. My past self would have laughed at this sentence, as I much greater preferred just staying alone, enjoying my things almost in secret.

But now, things have changed, and I feel the need of sharing about what I do and love with other enthusiasts.

 

So, if you also like motorcycling and everything around it, stay with me, and take part in the journey !
